    Introduce
    I am a final-year student at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, majoring in Physics with a secondary major on Artificial Intelligence and Data Analysis, and minoring in Mathematics. In the coming semester, I will be joining the Southern University of Science and Technology in Shenzhen as an exchange student, where I plan to take advanced courses in Statistics, Mathematics, and Physics.

    Academically, I am deeply passionate about theoretical and applied research, with a strong interest in pursuing a PhD in areas such as Theoretical Statistics, Machine Learning (particularly diffusion models), and Quantum Computing.

    While my university (academic) journey has been heavily focused on coursework and research, I have also actively participated in extracurricular activities, such as co-founding the Hainan-Hong Kong Youth Federation and volunteering in community service initiatives, including food distribution for the elderly. Additionally, I was part of a team that won second place in a entrepreneurship competition organized by the University of Macau, earning the opportunity to present our business proposal to representatives from eight major casinos.

    That said, my university life hasn’t been solely defined by academics or achievements. Recently, I went through an unexpected breakup with someone I deeply loved and saw a future with. This loss has been profoundly difficult, and I’m still in the process of healing and rebuilding myself emotionally.

    Although life feels somewhat off course right now, I haven’t let go of hope. More than anything, I wish we could find our way back to each other and rekindle what we shared. At the same time, I am trying to focus on self-improvement—strengthening my mind, advancing my studies, and growing through this period of change.

    What I’ve learned through this experience is that while some things are beyond our control, we can always choose how we respond. I am committed to moving forward with sincerity, resilience, and an open heart—whether in research or the quiet journey back to myself.

    I look forward to embracing new opportunities, collaborating with like-minded individuals, and continuing my journey toward becoming a thoughtful and innovative researcher.
